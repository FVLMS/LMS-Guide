Guide to LMS Email Templates

This guide provides an overview of automated email templates sent from the Learning Management System (LMS). Each entry details the email's trigger, purpose, intended recipients, and the template content. Placeholders (e.g., TRAINEE.FIRST.NAME) are indicated within the templates and are dynamically populated by the system.

1. Request Training

  • Email (Internal Name): Request training
  • Purpose (What): Informs approvers when users request training, providing details on what was requested and by whom.
  • Recipient (Who): Sent to approvers with users requesting training.

Email Template:

Subject: Request Training - Approver

Dear APPROVER.FIRST.NAME APPROVER.LAST.NAME:

TRAINEE.FIRST.NAME TRAINEE.LAST.NAME has requested approval for the following training item:
Title: TITLE
Class Description: TRAINING.DESCRIPTION
Training Hours: DURATION

Please use the following link to approve/deny the training listed above:
APPROVAL.LINK

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact your learning organization.

16. Forgot Password

  • Email (Internal Name): Cornerstone LMS Password Reset
  • Purpose (What): Provides a link to reset a forgotten password.
  • Recipient (Who): Internal/external users.

Email Template:

Subject: Forget Password ONLY ADMINS SEE THIS

(Note: The actual subject line visible to users is likely more user-friendly, e.g., "Password Reset Request")

Dear RECIPIENT.FIRST.NAME RECIPIENT.LAST.NAME,

We received a request from you to reset your password to the Cornerstone portal. You may reset your password by clicking the following link: PASSWORD.RESET.URL

Please be aware that this URL link is valid for 48 hours. After the 48-hour validity period, clicking this link will navigate you to a page on which you must enter your login name to generate and email a new link. You may also select the "Forgot Password?" link from the portal login page at any time to generate a new password reset URL.

18. Certification Period Overdue Notification (GICH)

  • Email (Internal Name): x days prior - GICH - learner
  • Purpose (What): Notifies GICH certification holders that their certification is nearing its outdate date.
  • Recipient (Who): Certification holders nearing overdue status.

Email Template:

Subject: CERTIFICATION.TITLE Outdating on CERTIFICATION.DUE.DATE - Notify us if you are no longer required to maintain this class

Hello CERTIFICATION.USER.FIRST.NAME CERTIFICATION.USER.LAST.NAME,

This is an automated courtesy notification email that your CERTIFICATION.TITLE is outdating on CERTIFICATION.DUE.DATE.

The Fairview policy "Compliance with Requirements for Employment" states that life support classes outdate at the end of the month.

If CERTIFICATION.TITLE is not required for your role, please ask your manager to contact DEPT-GICH-STAFF-EDUCATION@fairview.org to remove you from the LMS reports.

Managers, before contacting DEPT-GICH-STAFF-EDUCATION@fairview.org, please verify that CERTIFICATION.USER.FIRST.NAME CERTIFICATION.USER.LAST.NAME (RECIPIENT.USERNAME) does not have a dual position that requires CERTIFICATION.TITLE. View the GICH Life Support Calendar and register for available classes by either contacting your scheduling/staffing office or by contacting Staff Education.

This email may be disregarded if you have recently taken this class onsite or an outside location. You must provide Human Resources with a copy of the certification card.
